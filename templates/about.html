We are alumni of the National Univeristy of Computer and Emerging Science, Lahore, Pakistan.
We built this model as our undergraduate final year project to investigate the performance of novel deep learning methods for classifying politeness in online requests.

The model works by building recursive neural networks based on the parse structures of each sentence in a given request. Softmax probabilities from each neural network are averaged to make the final prediction. The model is trained on the Stanford Politeness Corpus (Danescu-Niculescu-Mizil et al. ACL 2013), uses 100d GloVe representations (Pennington et al. EMNLP 2014) and is trained using mini batch gradient descent with AdaGrad.
